MEDSYSTEM PROTOTYPE

Time: Nov. 2021
Course: Strategic Prototyping 
Instructor: Elise Co.

Brief:
 Strategic Prototyping will introduce students to the variety of thinking and making that go into deciding which parts of a system to prototype, and how. We will break complex concepts into smaller interactions, mechanisms and moments; evaluate which are most important based on the context of the prototype; consider the range of fidelity from low to high; and try different methods of prototyping, including paper, video, technological/functional, and wizard-of-oz. 

Audio-only Prototype

Draw a state diagram of the Technology Excerpt.

Select at least 2 MOMENTS (transition between at least 2 states) plus 3 additional STATES. Design audio for each. Consider: sustained/continuous sound vs. momentary(deterministic) sound; foreground vs. background; relationships between sounds and states that are similar vs radically different.

Hardware/Switch Prototype - Input+LED

Select one MOMENT from any of the three System Descriptions we’ve read in class so far (Predictor, Calliagnosia, MedSystem). Prototype it using ONE switch/button and ONE LED. This assignment is all about context! You must design and build an enclosure and/or environment in which the components are placed.
