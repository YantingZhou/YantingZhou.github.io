FLORA YANTING ZHOU

Heart of Water (Audio Visual)

Time: Mar-Apr. 2021
Type: Collaborative
Role: Animation/Audio Visualization
Tool: TouchDesigner, After Effect

Introduction
This is a thesis project of a student majoring in sound. She collaborated with me on the video production. I was responsible for creating a sound visualization video based on the sound footage she produced. The final work was exhibited at the China Academy of Art graduation exhibition in 2021.

Return to One

The aggregated particles reflect the concept of Daoism.

A Heart as Still as Water

The heart is as clear as water, and sees all the feelings of the world. 
--Liu Yuxi
