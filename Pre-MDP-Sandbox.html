SANDBOX NFT Characters

Time: May-June. 2021
Type: Individule
Tool: VoxEdit, Blender

Introduction
In the 2021 China Sandbox NFT competition, I submitted three entries, among which "CodingWarrior" won the fifth place and the prize of 1000 SAND.

Coding Warrior (Awarded)

A samurai wearing armor, holding a sharp blade, and wearing sunglasses that cannot cover the dark circles under his eyes. 
In the era of network application infrastructure, each soldier and programmer are on the cusp. The codes they composed symbolize their lives —Thousand years later, they will be a memorable group of silent donors in history. Different coding languages are double-edged sword, error# error# came silently without knowing when;

Nian - Monster of Year

996 and overtime, the impact of involution on the new generation of young people from time to time, day, year after year. The CCTV said: "Now every days are like Chinese New Year." Nian Beast, also known as "Xi", is an evil beast in ancient Han myths and legends. People set off firecrackers and post Fai chun to drive off the Nian Beast. Nowadays, it’s time for Nian to experience his 996 lifestyle : "People celebrate the New Year everyday, and I have to work overtime every night. Anyway, yesterday’s KPI did not meet the standard, see you at work.”

Baseball Girl

The baseball girl combed the head of the Chinese-style balls of the 19th century China, and the small bamboo basket in her hand was filled with baseball and hearts. At the end of the 19th century, baseball was first introduced by overseas students and returned overseas Chinese, and then became a whirlwind in the Republic of China. It became a diplomatic sport that united people's spirits and enriched lives. The girl plays with the baseball bat, with a playful posture, showing her confidence.
