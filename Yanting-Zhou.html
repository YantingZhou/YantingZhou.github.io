FLORA YANTING ZHOU
Flora
Yanting Zhou

UI/UX Designer / Multimedia Artist

2021 - 2024 
MFA Media Design Practices - ArtCenter College of Design
2016 - 2020
BFA Painting - Nanjing University of Arts

Flora Zhou is a practitioner, risk-taker, and perfectionist. As a painter, she learned to transcend traditional mediums, breaking the boundary between art, design, and emerging technologies.

With a focus on visual storytelling and concept development, Flora investigates a range of subjects including user experience, human-nature relationship, and synthetic biology. Her projects reflect a deep engagement with the human drive to "better" the world.

By forging partnerships with creative individuals, immersing herself in startup ventures, and aligning with forward-thinking collaborators, she masterfully blends practical design expertise with profound conceptual insights. This synergy elevates her work, setting a new standard for innovation and creativity in her field.
