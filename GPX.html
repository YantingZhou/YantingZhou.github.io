DISTANCE CLOCK

Time: Oct. 2021
Instructor: Tim Durfee

We will create a clock of space, rather than time. Using Strava, or any app that can export a GPS file, track your movements over a duration, speed, and location that we determine in class. ​In a workshop, we will combine our individual movements into a comprehensive 12-hour “clock.”
