ASCII Text webpage

Time: Jan. 2022
Course: Creative Technology 2
Instructor: Maxim Safioulline
Tool: HTML, Github

Project Brief:

Create a Webpage that would create visual texture or illustration using only text formatted with HTML. Look at Carl Andre's poems or ASCII art for ideas. Publish in your Github repo.

DESIGN PROCESS

  • At the beginning I tried to google for ASCII, this video on left helps a lot. It mentioned the history of ASCII, that it is older than imagined and used before photo was able to be uploaded to computers, there are already experiments in early 90s.
  • According to the video I found some ASCII generators. I made styled texts with these generator and tried to paste them into my html file, and I used <xmp> for reserving the white space between, or else it would be in a mess.
  • Then I want to add some picture or pattern into the webpage. I tried plenty of ASCII art generator website with photos, but the translated outcome are blurry and weak in contrast (maybe depends on the quality of picture), this was not what I was looking for. So I tried to draw some vectors in illustrator, and turn the purely B&W png to text using one of the sites which has white background.
