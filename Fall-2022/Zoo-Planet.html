FLORA YANTING ZHOU

Zoo Planet

Time: Oct. 2022
Course: Concept Studio 1
Instructor: Sean Donahue
Tool: Blender

TAKE US TO A PLACE

Using found, hosted online camera feeds and the technology introduced through our workshops, take us to a “place.” Your work should make clear the particular worldview/s of the place you take us to—how do your guests “see/view/participant” with these? This worldview will come from the observational work you’ve done over the course of the class in digital and hybrid “places”. 

Final Project Brief

Zoo Planet is an artificial landscape composed of live streams from different zoos. Engaging with the physical and digital aspect of animal carcerality.

DESIGN DOCUMENTATION

  • Nets, cages
  • Artificial natural habitat
  • Islands & Isolation
  • Donation for “protection”
  • Surveillance
  • Digital Carcerality
