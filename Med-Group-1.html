VIDEO POROTOTYPE

Time: Nov. 2021
Course: Strategic Prototyping
Instructor: Elise Co

Choose either Calliagnosia system (not just the helmet) or MedSystem technologies for a video prototype. Your video prototype should show how the technology works (especially how they are controlled and what their outputs/effects are).

Team Menber: Arianna Borgeson, Benjamine Glasser

DESIGN PROCESS

Narrative (Flora)

Write out a narrative (a scene) in which your system is being used (If you choose MedSystem, do not use the narrative excerpt - write your own). Think about specific MOMENTS that you need to show particular actions or implications (e.g. “he dies, and the system indicates he’s dead) 

Storyboard (Ben)

Storyboard your video prototype. Indicate where you would cut to different scenes, close-ups, context shots, reaction shots. Indicate if you would have any subtitles, text, graphical overlays.

Role-Play (Arianna)

ROLE-PLAY with your teammates - act out some of your key moments to help you figure out the details or implications for how something might work. Use props or low-fidelity (paper, cardboard) prototypes to help you role play. Document some of your role-playing
