FLORA YANTING ZHOU

Projection Mapping Workshop

Type:
Art Installation

Duration:
04.2023

Using a combination of found objects to create a 3D space... And use screen-based imagery and experiment with creating different spatial experiences using Madmapper.

How does the spatial experiment then re-inform the source material?

Course

Creative Technology 4

Specification

Projection Mapping
Generative Art

Tool

TouchDesigner
MadMapper

Inspiration

As someone who is fascinated by the patterns found in plants and fungi, I approached this project with a focus on the sensations and experiences associated with organic growth and co-existence.

Projection Material

To convey a sense of growth, I compiled a collection of various video footages, which includes previous coursework, stock footage, and TouchDesigner experiments.

Surfaces

Skull model
Whisky bottle
Painted canvas

Set Up

Target graphics onto the physical geometries using MadMapper

Output

Dreaming Afterlife 

Projecting imagery of life onto an object signifies the death.

New Born

Big bang theory of a human embryo...

World in a Bottle

A tiny cosmos composed of caustics, reflections, and motions.
