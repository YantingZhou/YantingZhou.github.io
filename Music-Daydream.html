Music Daydream

Time: Feb. 2022
Course: Video Production
Instructor: Kate Parsons
Tool: Final Cut Pro, Blender, Motion 5

Project Brief:
Produce an original short film from conception to realization using a reading from your class with Prof. Barbosa. Students are free to create any kind of moving image work, about any subject matter that adheres to the structures weâ€™ve outlined in class: Chronology, Mapping, Audio-driven, and Musical Structures are all acceptable. Some choose to use readings from Critical Histories II as inspiration. 

Criteria for a successful project are: 

- Follows the rules of basic videography (exposure, etc.) covered in class
- Sensitivity to editing, proper lighting and framing/composition
- A discernible and intentional structure
- Approximate length: 1-4 minutes

DESIGN PROCESS

Research & Experiment

At the beginning I downloaded various apps using LIDAR to experiment with different inputs and outputs. There are 3Dscanner using rear camera to create low poly model and ScandyPro using front camera to create delicate model. After comparing and analyzing I chose the latter for its intimacy.

I created large amount of models using my face, hands and other body parts and also tried to render them in different modes like monochrome, wireframes and normals.
